Conditional Contracts Training

There have been many recent cases on conditional contracts where it seems that the parties have great difficulty in drafting what they really want - and achieving certainty.

These are highly complex documents and this highly practical seminar will draw on the speaker's extensive experience in this area and review the latest cases to show you how to avoid the problems.

What You Will Learn

The course will cover the following:

  • TCondition precedent or subsequent?
  • Drafting landlord's consent conditions
  • Drafting planning conditions
  • Satisfying/waiving conditions
  • Longstop dates and termination
  • Best, reasonable and all reasonable endeavours - what do they mean?
  • Remedies - what to do when things go wrong
  • Land Registry issues
  • Practical drafting tips and lessons from the cases

In House Training

TRLPLAW In House training often offers a more cost effective and flexible solution if your organisation requires training for multiple delegates. An increasing number of organisations are looking for In House Training which is provided on their premises and tailored specifically for them whilst minimising the disruption and lost fee earning time.

Your organisation can benefit from customised versions of our public courses tailored to suit your particular needs. We also work regularly with clients to develop one off solutions and if you have a specific training requirement that requires bespoke training, we would be happy to help.
